Instructions

IMPORTANT: Please return this HIT if you have already played this game. Your HIT will not be approved if you have played before, and you will be compromising the integrity of the results we are obtaining from other participants.

After finishing the instructions, you will enter a waiting room for a game. Some games require multiple participants, so you may have to wait for other players to join before the game will start. You will receive a bonus at a constant rate of 12 cents per minute in the waiting room as long as you stay active. The maximum time you will spend in the waiting room is five minutes.

Once the game starts, you will see a screen that looks much like the following:

You will be in control of the blue triangle. When you are not holding down any keys, you will move at a slow speed. Holding down space bar will increase your speed. The left and right arrow keys will control the direction of your movement.

You will have a score at each point in the game, indicated by the percentage shown at the top of the page. Each location in the game area is associated with a certain score. Your goal is to locate yourself in high scoring locations for as long as possible. You will receive a cash bonus based on your cumulative score in the game. The scores of locations will change over time, so you will have to continuously be ready to move around to maximize your bonus.

You will also be able to see the other participants who are currently playing in your game. These players can also see you. The score at any particular location at any particular point in time is the same for all players, and there is no competition between players.

After the game ends, you will receive a survey code to submit with your HIT. You must submit the code exactly as shown to receive your bonus.

IMPORTANT: If you exit the game window or are otherwise detected as inactive in the waiting room or in the game, you will be removed from the game and your HIT may not be approved. In order to remain active, you must stay off the walls in the game. When you are touching a wall, your score will become a red "0%".

Players occasionally face bugs during gameplay. In the rare case that you think you are experiencing unexpected game behavior and you are not removed from the game automatically after a short time, please make a note of your current URL, take a screenshot, and message me.
